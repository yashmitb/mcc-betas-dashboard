Methodology

Sources, assumptions & data quality

📊 Asset beta formula

Column "Inferred aß" uses: aß = eß × (1 – Gearing).

CEER stated betas use the full Hamada formula: aß = eß ÷ [1+(1–t)×(D/E)]. The "Check" column (in the xlsx) shows the discrepancy. For exact values, use the stated figure from column I.

🔧 Processing assumptions

  • Zero equity betas → blank (countries that didn't report)
  • Ranges (Ireland "0.78–0.89") → midpoints (0.835)
  • Multi-value betas ("old/new assets") → first numeric value
  • Country year suffixes stripped ("GB (2022)" → "GB")
  • European comma decimals ("0,28") → 0.28
  • Bare percentages ("44.62") → decimal (0.4462)

⚠ Data quality flags

N. Macedonia (MK): Stated asset beta (0.66) > equity beta (0.41) in RFR24/25. Mathematically impossible under standard finance — likely an error in the CEER source. Treat with caution.

Albania (AL) E DSO: Equity beta = 6.58 from 2010 historical data. Extreme outlier in CEER source. Excluded from European averages in charts.
